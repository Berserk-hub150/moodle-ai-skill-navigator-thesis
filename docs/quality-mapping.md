# Quality Attribute Mapping

## Maintainability

Small service classes and explicit responsibilities.

## Reusability

AI provider and workflow services are reused by tutor, quiz, mind map and XR modules.

## Portability

Provider, endpoint and model are configurable.

## Robustness

Prototype provider keeps the demo usable without external AI.

## Verifiability

Service classes can be linted and tested independently from page rendering.

## Comprehensibility

Pattern documentation explains the role of each component.
